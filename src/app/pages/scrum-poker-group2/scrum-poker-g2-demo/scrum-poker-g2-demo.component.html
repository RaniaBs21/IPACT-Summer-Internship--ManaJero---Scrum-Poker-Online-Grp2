Limits of Planning Poker?‚ö°

Create an instant room

Creating an instant room in Planning Poker is a streamlined process designed to facilitate quick and efficient agile estimation sessions for remote or distributed teams. This process involves setting up a virtual space where team members can collaborate in real-time to estimate user stories.


Add/Import UserStories

Ensure that the user stories or tasks to be estimated are well-defined and understood by the team. This might involve discussing the acceptance criteria and any dependencies or constraints.


Invite your team members

Include all relevant team members such as developers, testers, product owners, and other stakeholders who have a good understanding of the work to be estimated.


Vote on task complexity

Once everyone has selected their card, all team members reveal their chosen cards simultaneously. This step is crucial to prevent any bias or influence during the estimation process. It ensures that each estimate is based on the individual's understanding of the user story.


Discussion

Communicate with your team in real-time. We have a built-in chat feature that allows you to communicate with your team members. The comments feature allows you to leave comments on the current ticket, ensuring you only see the comments that are relevant to the current planning poker topic.
