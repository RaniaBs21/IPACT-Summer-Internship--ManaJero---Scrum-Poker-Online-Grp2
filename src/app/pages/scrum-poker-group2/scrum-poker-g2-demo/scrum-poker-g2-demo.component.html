How it works ?‚ö°üöÄ












Join the session

The team members gather to participate in a planning poker session. This is typically done during a sprint planning meeting where all relevant team members are present.

Discussing the User Story

The product owner or a team member explains the user story to ensure everyone understands the requirements and scope of the task. This helps in having a common understanding of what needs to be estimated.

Secret Selection of Cards

Each team member privately selects a card that represents their estimate for the user story. The card values typically follow a Fibonacci sequence or similar scale to represent the effort or complexity.


Displaying All the Cards at Once

After everyone has made their selection, all cards are revealed simultaneously. This ensures that individual estimates are not influenced by others.

Discuss Highest and Lowest Estimates

The team discusses the highest and lowest estimates to understand the reasoning behind these selections. This step is crucial for uncovering different perspectives and potential misunderstandings.

Consensus?

The team evaluates whether there is a consensus on the estimate. If there is a consensus, the estimate is finalized. If not, further discussion is needed.


Success

If consensus is reached, the estimation process for the user story is completed successfully, and the team can move on to the next user story.
