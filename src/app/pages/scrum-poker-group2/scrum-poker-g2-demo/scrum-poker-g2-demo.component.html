What are the Planning Poker steps ?
1. Set The Stage

The product owner or Scrum master will act as the moderator,he will create the room, plannified a session and add team members. all team members will play, and there will be plenty of room for discussion and questions throughout the session.


2. Hand Out The Poker Cards

The Scrum master will choose the algorithm that match his project before starting the session and that's how each member of the team will have the same cards


3. Import UserStories from JIRA

Elevate your planning poker sessions with our Jira integration! You effortlessly import your tickets from Jira, and have the results automatically synced back!.
Setup our integration with just a few clicks, and then you can use our advanced search to find and import your tickets.
We know know how painful it can be to manually update your tickets while planning, so we made it easy for you!


4. Participate to session

The first step in Planning Poker for all team members is to join the estimation session.
This typically occurs during a sprint planning meeting where the entire team is present. The purpose is to ensure everyone has a common understanding of the work that needs to be estimated.


5. Play cards

Once everyone has selected their card, all team members reveal their chosen cards simultaneously.
This step is crucial to prevent any bias or influence during the estimation process.
It ensures that each estimate is based on the individual's understanding of the user story.


6. Communicate

Communicate with your team in real-time. We have a built-in chat feature that allows you to communicate with your team members.
The comments feature allows you to leave comments on the current ticket, ensuring you only see the comments that are relevant to the current planning poker topic.


7. Download Report

OWe have Analytics at every level! View how your team is voting for each ticket, each session, or for all of your sessions inside of a Space!
We provide you with the tools to help you understand your teams estimates, and improve your planning sessions, with detailed analytics.
